Alert

Use alerts to give users feedback after they complete an action, or to draw their attention to important information.

There are four variants — information, success, warning and error. See the MOJ Design System for full guidance.

Information

Example
{{ mojAlert({ variant: "information", title: "Calculations are being run in the background. The case will be ready to review shortly.", showTitleAsHeading: false }) }} 
{% raw %}{{ mojAlert({
  variant: "information",
  title: "Calculations are being run in the background. The case will be ready to review shortly."
}) }}{% endraw %}

Example

', showTitleAsHeading: true }) }}
