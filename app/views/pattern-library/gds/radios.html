Radios

Use the radios component when users can only select one option from a list.

Default radios

Used on review and decision screens throughout the casework flow — for example selecting Accepted, Information requested, or Cannot accept.

Example

What is your decision?

 
<div class="govuk-form-group">
  <fieldset class="govuk-fieldset">
    <legend class="govuk-fieldset__legend govuk-fieldset__legend--m">
      <h2 class="govuk-fieldset__heading">What is your decision?</h2>
    </legend>
    <div class="govuk-radios" data-module="govuk-radios">
      <div class="govuk-radios__item">
        <input class="govuk-radios__input" id="decision" name="decision" type="radio" value="Accepted">
        <label class="govuk-label govuk-radios__label" for="decision">Accepted</label>
      </div>
      <div class="govuk-radios__item">
        <input class="govuk-radios__input" id="decision-2" name="decision" type="radio" value="Information requested">
        <label class="govuk-label govuk-radios__label" for="decision-2">Information requested</label>
      </div>
    </div>
  </fieldset>
</div>

Conditionally revealing content (progressive reveal)

Reveal an extra field only when the radio it relates to is selected, so users are not shown content they do not need. Add data-aria-controls to the input and a govuk-radios__conditional container with the matching id; the radios JS (data-module="govuk-radios") handles showing and hiding. Keep the revealed content short — usually a single related field.

Example

What is your decision?

 
<div class="govuk-radios" data-module="govuk-radios">
  <div class="govuk-radios__item">
    <input class="govuk-radios__input" id="dec-2" name="dec" type="radio"
           value="Information requested" data-aria-controls="conditional-dec-2">
    <label class="govuk-label govuk-radios__label" for="dec-2">Information requested</label>
  </div>
  <div class="govuk-radios__conditional govuk-radios__conditional--hidden" id="conditional-dec-2">
    <div class="govuk-form-group">
      <label class="govuk-label" for="info-needed">What information do you need from the applicant?</label>
      <textarea class="govuk-textarea" id="info-needed" name="info-needed" rows="3"></textarea>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>
