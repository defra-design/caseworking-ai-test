Inset text

Use the inset text component to differentiate a block of text from the content that surrounds it.

Use it for information that relates to but is not the main purpose of the surrounding content — for example a policy note or constraint that applies to a particular decision.

Do not use inset text to highlight or draw attention to important information — use warning text for that. In user research, people sometimes read inset text as less important rather than more, and skip over it.

Default inset text

Example

The maximum area that can be entered for this action is limited by the total eligible area of the land parcel. Check the land parcel details before proceeding.

<div class="govuk-inset-text">
  <p class="govuk-body">
    The maximum area that can be entered for this action is limited
    by the total eligible area of the land parcel.
  </p>
</div>
