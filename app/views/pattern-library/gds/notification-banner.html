Notification banner

Use a notification banner to tell the user about something they need to know about, but that's not directly related to the thing they're trying to do.

Information banner

The default blue banner is used for important information the caseworker should be aware of before proceeding.

Example

Important

A SSSI consent is required from Natural England before this application can proceed.

<div class="govuk-notification-banner" role="region"
     aria-labelledby="govuk-notification-banner-title"
     data-module="govuk-notification-banner">
  <div class="govuk-notification-banner__header">
    <h2 class="govuk-notification-banner__title" id="govuk-notification-banner-title">
      Important
    </h2>
  </div>
  <div class="govuk-notification-banner__content">
    <p class="govuk-body">A SSSI consent is required from Natural England.</p>
  </div>
</div>

Success banner

The green success banner confirms an action has completed — for example a note has been saved or a decision has been submitted.

Example

Success

Decision submitted

The application has been accepted and moved to the agreement stage.

<div class="govuk-notification-banner govuk-notification-banner--success"
     role="alert"
     aria-labelledby="govuk-notification-banner-title-success"
     data-module="govuk-notification-banner">
  <div class="govuk-notification-banner__header">
    <h2 class="govuk-notification-banner__title" id="govuk-notification-banner-title-success">
      Success
    </h2>
  </div>
  <div class="govuk-notification-banner__content">
    <h3 class="govuk-notification-banner__heading">Decision submitted</h3>
    <p class="govuk-body">The application has been accepted.</p>
  </div>
</div>
